Cars 1

While traveling to California to race against The King and Chick Hicks for the Piston Cup Championship, Lightning
McQueen becomes lost after falling out of his trailer in a run down town called Radiator Springs. While there he slowly
befriends the town's odd residents, including Sally, Doc Hudson and Mater. When it comes time
for him to leave to championship is no longer his top priority.
